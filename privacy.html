Privacy Policy for Lexi Calendar

Lexi Calendar is a personal-use application created for Jason to connect Google Calendar with OpenClaw.

Information accessed by this app may include calendar metadata and event details necessary to read, create, update, or delete calendar events at the user’s request.

How information is used

  • To display calendar events
  • To check availability and scheduling conflicts
  • To create, update, or delete calendar events requested by the user

Data sharing

  • No data is sold
  • No data is shared with third parties except as required to operate the Google Calendar connection requested by the user

Data retention

  • OAuth credentials and related tokens may be stored locally in Jason’s OpenClaw environment in order to maintain access without repeated sign-in
  • Calendar data is used only for the user’s requested actions

Security

  • Access is intended only for Jason’s personal use
  • Credentials are stored locally and managed by the user’s OpenClaw environment
